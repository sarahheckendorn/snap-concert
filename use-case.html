Use Case

Hannah wants to easily find and review venues where her favorite concerts have been held.

Interaction Flow

  • Hannah opens her browser on her mobile and enters URL
  • Server takes information and returns site
  • Site asks for zip code to pull up concerts in her area
  • Hannah scrolls through list of recent concerts in her area and selects one
  • Site pulls up page
  • Hannah scrolls down to review box at the bottom, types her review, and taps the 'submit' button.
  • Site takes information and posts it to the page.
  • Back to Persona
  • Back to Navigation
